Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. At Antonio Murrieta Apps, we manage your data responsibly, including how we collect, use, and store it.

What we collect:

  • Personal information such as your email, name, and usage data to enhance our services.
  • Device information and app usage statistics for performance optimization.
  • Crash reports and error logs to improve app stability.

How we use it:

  • To improve user experiences, provide support, and introduce new features.
  • To send important updates about app functionality and security.
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize app performance.

Your rights:

  • You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data anytime by contacting create@antoniomurrieta.com.

  • You have the right to data portability and to withdraw consent at any time.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug?

Please email us at create@antoniomurrieta.com with a detailed description of the issue, including steps to reproduce it, your device model, and app version.

How often are apps updated?

We release updates regularly to fix bugs, improve performance, and add new features. Enable automatic updates to always have the latest version.

Is my data backed up?

Yes, we provide automatic cloud backup for your app data. You can manage backup settings within the app.

Can I use the apps offline?

Most features work offline, but some functionality requires an internet connection for synchronization and updates.
